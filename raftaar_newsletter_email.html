Laying the Tracks: The Thinking Behind Raftaar

Over the past few months, our executive committee has been working to deeply understand how consumer expectations, retail formats, and logistics models are evolving — not just in India, but globally. In this effort, we have been supported by a dedicated team from the Boston Consulting Group (BCG), an international management consulting firm.

This strategic partnership has helped us shape Raftaar with a foundation of deeply understanding what customers want, global benchmarks, and a sharp focus on execution. Together, we've identified a massive opportunity: over $2 billion in logistics value creation by 2030 — fueled by a digital-first consumer base that's rapidly expanding beyond metros, and an urgent need for speed, reliability, and hyperlocal service delivery.

Raftaar is our response to this shift. We're not here to play catch-up — we're here to define the space. We're combining DTDC's unmatched reach with a new generation of technology, talent, and operating models to build a rapid commerce backbone. To do this, we are building a convenient and cost-effective solution for our customers, offering sub-same-day delivery that can serve everything from emerging D2C brands to large-format retailers, from spare parts to beauty essentials.

It is our hope that this exciting new chapter with Raftaar will be instrumental in shaping not just DTDC, but the very future of logistics in India.

This newsletter is an effort to share our wins and learnings with the DTDC family. You'll find real updates from the field — launches, pilots, operations, tech wins, and team moments — as we go live, city by city.

Thanks for being part of what we believe is just the beginning.

— AJAY SUKHWANI

04

What's happening in the Raftaar Arena?

Bringing together our leaders: Building Raftaar, Together

To bring Raftaar to life, DTDC leaders from across functions and locations came together for an intensive, hands-on workshop series focused on defining how Raftaar will operate — end to end.

From dream store setup to last-mile execution, critical workflows were debated, defined, and documented — laying the foundation for our rapid launches in Vasai, Nagpur, and Rajarhat.

This was DTDC at its best: agile, aligned, and action-oriented.

A special shoutout to the DTDC leadership team for their active sponsorship and partnership in this workshop.

Further workshops are already being planned to finalize channel engagement strategies and sales activation frameworks as we gear up for Raftaar's next wave of scale.

Where We Build: Turning Branches into Raftaar Hubs

As part of our rapid expansion planning, a structured branch space assessment was conducted across key DTDC locations to identify underutilized areas that could be transformed into Raftaar Dream Stores.

45,000+ sq. ft. of potentially utilizable space was mapped across 24+ branches in cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore, and Pune.

Comprehensive evaluations included operational zones, basements, mezzanine levels, and cabins — with focus on accessibility and year-round usability.

Data-driven approach enabled fast-tracked setup in cities like Vasai, Nagpur, Agra, Kanpur and Rajarhat, and is now supporting upcoming launches in Delhi, Bangalore and more.

The space assessment effort reflects how Raftaar is building fast without building new — optimizing existing infrastructure to drive scale with speed.
